Please read these instructions carefully to understand how to make your allocations:
  • Using the Slider:
    • You will see a slider on your screen for each round.
    • This slider determines your allocation between Lottery 1 (L1) and Lottery 2 (L2).
    • Moving the slider adjusts the percentage allocated to L1. The remaining percentage is automatically allocated to L2.
  • Understanding the Allocations:
    • The slider values range from 0.01 (1%) to 1.00 (100%).
    • For example:
      • A slider value of 0.3 means 30% in L1 and 70% in L2.
      • A slider value of 0.75 means 75% in L1 and 25% in L2.
      • A slider value of 1.0 means 100% in L1 and 0% in L2.
    • You can adjust the slider in increments of 0.01 (1%) for precise allocations.
  • Important Rules:
    • The minimum allocation to L1 is 1% (0.01 on the slider).
    • You cannot allocate 0% to L1 - selecting 0 is not permitted.
  • Making Your Decision:
    • Consider the probabilities and potential outcomes for each lottery carefully.
    • Adjust the slider until you're satisfied with your allocation between L1 and L2.
    • Once you've made your decision, you can proceed to the next round.
