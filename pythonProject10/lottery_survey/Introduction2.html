  • Probability and Outcomes:
    • With probability p: L1 decreases to its low value, L2 increases to its high value
    • With probability (1-p): L1 increases to its high value, L2 decreases to its low value
    • The exact probabilities and values will be shown to you in each round.
    • Pay close attention to these numbers as they will change from round to round.
  • Rounds and Payment:
    • The experiment consists of 45 rounds. Each round is independent.
    • At the end of the experiment, one round will be randomly selected for payment.
    • Your earnings will be based on your allocation decision in that round and the random outcome.
    • Think carefully about each decision, as any round could be the one that determines your payment.

    • Remember: There are no right or wrong answers. We are interested in understanding your personal decision-making process in these scenarios.
